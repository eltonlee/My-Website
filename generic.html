Pick an Ingredient

Cross platform mobile application

Introduction

“Pick an Ingredient” is an android app focused on recommending daily recipes based on users’ diet type, preferred ingredients, and most importantly, nutrition intakes.

It was designed for people who want or need a specific diet and minimizes the time and work needed to pick appropriate recipes and to calculate all the nutrition intakes.

This mobile application was created by a team of 5 people.

Technologies Used

  • Python
  • C#
  • Xamarin.Forms
  • Google Firebase
  • Xcode
  • Visual Studios
